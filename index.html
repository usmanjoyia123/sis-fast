Welcome to the SIS Research Group at FAST NUCES.

About

A research group focused on designing data-driven, human-centric intelligent systems by integrating machine learning, IoT, and cross domain adaptive technologies for real-world applications.

Introduction

The Smart & Intelligent Systems (SIS) Research Group focuses on the development of advanced intelligent systems that combine machine learning, IoT, and data analytics to address real-world challenges. The group emphasizes human-centric design, enabling systems that support decision making, adapt to user needs, and operate in dynamic environments. SIS aims to contribute to emerging areas such as Industry 5.0, smart environments, and personalized intelligent services, while fostering interdisciplinary collaboration and industry engagement.

Research Areas

  • Intelligent Decision Systems
  • Smart Systems & IoT
  • Industry 5.0
  • Predictive Analytics
